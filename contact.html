Frequently Asked Questions

💨 What's the delivery time?

Our standard delivery time is 15-20 minutes from order placement. You'll receive real-time tracking updates via SMS.

💳 What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Cash on Delivery, UPI, Credit/Debit Cards, and Net Banking. Choose what's most convenient for you!

🚚 Is there a delivery charge?

No! Delivery is completely free on orders above ₹199. Orders below ₹199 have a ₹30 delivery fee.

📦 How can I track my order?

You'll receive a tracking link via email and SMS immediately after placing your order. Track it live on our website!

🔄 What's your return policy?

You can return items within 24 hours for a full refund. No questions asked! Contact us for details.

🎁 What promo codes are available?

New users get DROP40 for 40% off their first order. Check our website for other ongoing offers!

❌ What if I'm not available for delivery?

Let your delivery partner know beforehand. They'll try again within 30 minutes or you can reschedule delivery.

📱 Do you have a mobile app?

Our mobile app is coming soon! It will have exclusive app-only deals and faster checkout. Stay tuned!

🌍 Do you deliver everywhere?

We currently deliver to all major hostels in the city. Check your hostel in the dropdown to confirm delivery availability.

🎉 Got any feedback?

We'd love to hear from you! Your feedback helps us improve our service.

Still have questions?

Our support team is always ready to help!
